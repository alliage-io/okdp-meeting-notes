- [OKDP#15](https://github.com/OKDP/OKDP/issues/15): Discussions around merge strategy (CODEOWNERS, teams, branch rules). It was decided to create teams at the company level, as well as additional teams at the working group level, which may include several people from different companies. The naming convention is as follow:

  - `cn-{company}` for a company level team.
  - `wg-{subject}` for working group level.

  It was also decided to merge pull requests using the `squash-merge` strategy.
