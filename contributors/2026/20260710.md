## Open topics

- OKDP Agnostic Philosophy & Component Lifecycle: The core philosophy of OKDP is to remain completely cloud-and-storage-agnostic, allowing users to seamlessly connect their own storage or services to the data platform.
  - Currently, the `SeaweedFS` component is located in the `platform-packages` repository, which incorrectly implies that it is an official part of the OKDP core and will be actively supported by contributors.
  - To align with the project's philosophy, it has been decided to remove such components from `platform-packages` and relocate them to a separate repository, as they are still required for the `okdp-sandbox` environment. Adaltas will take the lead on this migration.
