## Open topics

- Some newly created issues are using the `Blank issue` option instead of the predefined templates, which should be avoided except in very specific scenarios. The official templates utilize structured forms to streamline formatting and ensure all necessary context is provided. If a specific field does not apply to your case, please fill it in with `N/A`. While the web form itself isn't strictly mandatory, adhering to the template structure is mandatory. If you must use a `Blank issue`, ensure you manually follow the required template format.
- **Binary Publishing & Regulatory Responsibility**: Ongoing discussions are happening regarding who will take responsibility for publishing the OKDP binaries. Under European legislation, entities publishing binaries for commercial purposes are classified as "manufacturers." This status legally holds them responsible for the security of the product as well as all of its underlying dependencies.
- **Octave**: Conducted a presentation and live demonstration of OIDC DCR (Dynamic Client Registration) The objective is to automate and simplify client registration with the IDP (Keycloak Identity Provider) via a secure key exchange. Since not all technologies natively support this registration method, custom adaptations may sometimes be required to make it work.
